About

This tool helps musicians explore variation techniques in Irish traditional music, with special consideration for Anglo concertina playability.

Variation Tiers:

  • Traditional: Session-standard techniques used for 100+ years
  • Extended Traditional: Modern session techniques (cross-rhythm, drones)
  • Contemporary: Jazz-influenced (quartal harmony, chromatic passing tones)
  • Experimental: Avant-garde deconstruction and exploration
